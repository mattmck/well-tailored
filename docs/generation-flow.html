Resume
resumeSystemPrompt()
resumeUserPrompt(input)
complete(model, sys, usr)
"You are an expert resume writer. Your job is to tailor the candidate's base resume for a specific role at a specific company…"
NEVER invent, fabricate, or exaggerate experience, credentials, or achievements
Every bullet must read like someone who owned the outcome — use "built", "shipped", "led" — never "helped" or "assisted"
Select Impact bullets: the 2–3 achievements that most directly map to this job description's stated priorities
LENGTH — must fit on one page when rendered as PDF. Be ruthless
↺ up to 6 retries on 429
exponential backoff · 30s base
Cover Letter
coverLetterSystemPrompt()
coverLetterUserPrompt(input)
complete(model, sys, usr)
"You are a cover letter writer who specializes in sharp, confident, non-generic writing for senior engineers…"
Tone: sharp, confident, and dangerous — the candidate sounds like someone who could walk away from this offer
Prohibited: "I'm excited to apply", "I believe my background aligns", "I would love the opportunity", "team player"
Paragraph 3: name a specific mission, product, or technical challenge from this company — not a generic compliment
NEVER invent or infer experience not explicitly stated in the resume or bio
↺ up to 6 retries on 429
exponential backoff · 30s base
